Create a Business Plan

The following are key aspects that every business plan should include:

The Executive Summary – a 30 second pitch that outlines your business.

Market Analysis – How big of a role you intent to play and why.

Company Description – An overview of what the business will do and why it is viable.

Organization & Management – Outline of the experience and expertise the company has.

Marketing & Sales – Your strategy for customer acquisition and how it will be done.

Products or Services – What product is the company going to develop or offer.

Capitalization Requirements – How much funding is required and how will it be raised.

Financials – At least 2 years of projected sales revenue that the industry and market can support.
